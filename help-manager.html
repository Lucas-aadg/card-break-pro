What You Can Access

Your owner gave you a Manager invite code. You signed up through the Join page, selected Manager, and now have your own dashboard. Here's exactly what you have access to:

✓ You have access to

  • Schedule — create & manage shifts
  • Inventory — add, edit, restock products
  • Inventory Log — full audit trail
  • Sort Queue — monitor & update tasks
  • Reports — dashboard, stream summary, break history, product ROI
  • Performance — breaker & sorter metrics
  • Team — view all members
  • Settings — your own profile, email, password

✗ Owner-only

  • Payroll & pay rate settings
  • Billing & subscription
  • Generating invite codes
  • Reset data
  • Break entry (live streaming)
  • Commission settings
📅

Schedule

  1. 1
    Create a shift

    Use the form at the top of the Schedule tab. Select the date, choose a breaker or sorter from the dropdown, set a start and end time, and add an optional stream key. Hit Create Shift — it appears on the calendar and in the team member's Shifts tab immediately.

  2. 2
    Calendar view

    The calendar highlights days that have shifts scheduled. Click any highlighted day to jump to that week's shifts in the upcoming table.

  3. 3
    Edit or cancel a shift

    In the Upcoming Shifts table, click Edit to change the date, time, or stream key. Click Cancel to remove it — the team member will no longer see it in their dashboard.

  4. 4
    Clock Log

    Scroll down past the upcoming shifts table to see a full log of every clock-in and clock-out across all team members. Filter by breaker or sorter. Use this to verify hours for the owner's payroll run.

📦

Inventory

  1. 1
    Add a new product

    Fill in the product name, cost per box, category, and starting stock. If you want the system to track a weighted average cost when you restock at different prices, check "Track weighted avg cost on restock". Click Add Product.

  2. 2
    Edit or restock

    Click Edit on any product. You can update the name, cost, category, or stock level. To restock, enter the quantity you're adding and the price you paid per box — the system recalculates the weighted average cost automatically.

  3. 3
    Filter by category

    Use the category pills above the product table to filter by sport or product type. Helps when you have a large inventory.

  4. 4
    Inventory Log

    Every stock change — initial adds, restocks, and usage from breaks — is recorded in the Inventory Log tab. Filter by time period, action type, or specific product. The summary strip at the top shows total units in, out, and net change for the selected period.

📬

Sort Queue

  1. 1
    What you see

    Every sort task for your organization, across all streams. Filter by status — All, Pending, In Progress, or Completed. Each row shows the stream, assigned sorter, break count, and current status.

  2. 2
    Monitor progress

    Use this view to see what's backed up and what's done. If something has been sitting in Pending for too long, follow up with the assigned sorter or check the Schedule tab to see if they have a shift today.

Note: Sort tasks are created automatically when a stream closes out. Sorters update their own status from their dashboard — you don't need to touch them unless you're monitoring or following up.
📊

Reports

  1. 1
    Dashboard

    High-level stats — total revenue, profit, breaks, streams, average profit per break, and top breakers. Shows recent break activity at a glance.

  2. 2
    Stream Summary

    Select a specific stream to see its full breakdown — total revenue, profit, box cost, platform fee, and each individual break in that stream.

  3. 3
    Break History

    Filter by date range to see every individual break logged. Includes breaker name, product, revenue, profit, and time. Useful for reviewing specific sessions.

  4. 4
    Product ROI

    Ranked table of all products showing total revenue, box cost, profit, and ROI percentage. Tells you exactly which boxes are worth breaking and which aren't.

🏆

Performance

  1. 1
    Breaker Performance

    Default view when you open Performance. Shows all breakers ranked by total revenue. Columns include: Breaks, Streams, Total Revenue, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Avg Revenue per Break, and Avg Profit per Break. Top 3 get gold/silver/bronze rankings.

  2. 2
    Sorter Performance

    Click the Sorters button at the top right to switch views. Shows all sorters ranked by tasks completed. Columns include: Tasks Done, Streams Sorted, Shifts Worked, Hours Worked, Avg Tasks per Shift, and Avg Hours per Shift.

Tip: Use Performance data when talking to your owner about scheduling decisions, recognizing strong performers, or identifying who might need support.
👥

Team

  1. 1
    See the full team roster

    The Team tab lists every member of your organization — name, role (breaker, sorter, or manager), email, and how many shifts they've worked in the last 30 days.

  2. 2
    What you don't see

    Pay rates, commission settings, and payout history are owner-only. If you need to reference pay information, ask your owner directly.

⚙️

Settings

  1. 1
    Profile

    Update your display name and phone number. Your name shows on schedules and in the team list.

  2. 2
    Change Email

    Update your account email. Confirmation links are sent to both addresses before the change takes effect.

  3. 3
    Change Password

    Enter a new password to update your login credentials. You'll stay logged in after saving.

Note: Billing and reset data are not available to managers. Contact your owner for subscription or data management needs.
