The sorting & shipping layer
Breaks shipped fast. Every time.

Card Break Pro has a built-in sorter role — the person who packages and ships cards to buyers after each stream. They get their own login, their own queue, and you get full visibility on where every order stands.

What Sorters See

  • → Oldest first, always. Streams are queued from oldest to newest — priority is built in so the right orders ship first and buyers aren't left waiting.
  • → Break count per stream. Each queue card shows the stream name, date, and how many breaks need to be sorted — so they always know the size of the job before they start.
  • → Start Sorting / Mark Complete. One tap to go In Progress, one tap to complete. Status updates instantly — you see it on your end in real time.
  • → Their own schedule. Clock in/out tied to their assigned shifts. Full time log, same as breakers.

Common Issues & Quick Fixes

Can't log in / invalid credentials
Make sure autocorrect isn't changing your password on mobile. Try copying and pasting it instead of typing. If you registered manually, your email may need to be confirmed — email us and we'll fix it instantly.
Invite code shows "Unknown" instead of business name
This has been fixed. Make sure you're on the latest version — hard refresh with Ctrl + Shift + R.
Sort queue not showing streams
Streams only appear in the sort queue after they've been closed out. If you have old streams, email us and we can backfill them manually.
Billing or subscription issues
Visit your billing portal from the owner dashboard, or email us directly at hello@cardbreakpro.com and we'll sort it out.
Something else entirely
Just email us. Describe what happened, what you expected, and what you saw. Screenshots help. We'll get back to you fast.
