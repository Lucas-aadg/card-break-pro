Common questions
Every question answered.
Do I need to connect my Whatnot account?
No. Card Break Pro works alongside Whatnot, Fanatics, TikTok Shop — or any platform. Your breaker enters the running total from whatever platform they're on. No integrations, no API keys, no setup.
Can one breaker work multiple channels?
Yes. Assign any team member to as many channels as you want. They'll see the products for whichever channel they're entered into. Sorters are shared across all channels automatically.
How does the per-break pay work?
Set a flat rate per break for sorters, or per-category rates for breakers — Disney breaks pay more than Football because they take longer. The system knows which category a product belongs to and calculates pay automatically when payroll is run.
What if someone forgets to clock out?
Your manager (or owner) can edit any clock-in or clock-out time directly from the Clock Log. Just click Edit on any shift row and fix it. Payroll recalculates automatically.
Is inventory tracking really automatic?
Yes. Every time a breaker submits a break, the products they used are deducted from stock instantly. When you restock, enter the boxes received and the batch price — the system blends it with existing stock using weighted average pricing.
Can I run payroll for any time period?
Any period — today, this week, last two weeks, this month, custom range. The system reads clock-in/out records, closed streams, and completed sort tasks to calculate every person's exact earnings. Mark as paid and it's logged forever.
Is there really no per-seat pricing?
None. $250/month flat — whether you have 3 team members or 30. Add as many breakers, sorters, and managers as your operation needs. The cost never changes.
What happens after the free trial?
After 14 days we'll ask for a payment method. If you're not ready to subscribe, cancel — no charge, no hard feelings. Your data stays intact for 30 days if you change your mind.
