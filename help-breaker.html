Breaker Help Guide

Quick guide to using Card Break Pro as a breaker. Read this once and you'll know everything.

🔑

Getting Started

  1. 1
    You got an invite code from your manager

    Go to the login page and click "Breaker? Join with invite code". Enter the code, set your name, email, and password. Done — your account is linked to your manager's business.

  2. 2
    Log in before your shift

    Sign in at the login page. If you have a shift today, you'll see a banner at the top reminding you. Click Clock In when you're ready to start.

📡

Break Entry — During Your Stream

  1. 1
    Enter your Stream Key

    This is the Whatnot event ID or the name your manager gave the stream. It ties your breaks to the right session. If you clocked in from a scheduled shift, it may already be pre-filled.

  2. 2
    Select your products

    Find each box you're breaking in the product table and set the quantity to 1 (or however many you're opening). Use the category pills at the top to filter by sport if you have a lot of products. The box cost and break even will update automatically.

  3. 3
    Check your Break Even

    The blue banner at the top shows you two numbers — the total you need to bring in to not lose money, and what that is per spot sold. Know this before you go live. It's based on your box cost and platform fee.

  4. 4
    Enter spots and platform fee %

    Spots = how many spots you sold in this break. Platform Fee = Whatnot's cut (usually around 8%). Ask your manager if you're unsure.

  5. 5
    After each break, enter the Whatnot running total and submit

    Look at the total sales number on your Whatnot screen and type it in the revenue field. Do NOT try to calculate each break separately — the app handles that automatically by subtracting from your previous total.

  6. 6
    Click SUBMIT BREAK

    Each time you open a new box and the sale goes through on Whatnot, submit a break. You'll see it log in the running total on the right side of the screen.

  7. 7
    At the end — Close Out Stream

    When your stream ends, enter the final Whatnot sales total and click CLOSE OUT STREAM. This saves everything, calculates your payout (if commissions are on), and automatically clocks you out.

Important: Always type the cumulative Whatnot total — not the amount just sold. For example: after Break 1 you type $200, after Break 2 you type $380, after Break 3 you type $510. The app figures out each break's revenue.
📅

My Shifts

  1. 1
    See your upcoming schedule

    The My Shifts tab shows all shifts your manager has assigned to you. Today's shifts are highlighted with a TODAY badge. You'll also see a reminder banner on the break entry screen when you have a shift today.

  2. 2
    Clock In from the banner

    When you're ready to go live, click the green Clock In button in the shift reminder banner at the top of Break Entry. This marks your shift as In Progress and pre-fills your stream key.

  3. 3
    Past shifts

    Scroll down in My Shifts to see a table of your completed and cancelled shifts, including clock in and clock out times.

💰

My Payouts

  1. 1
    View your earnings

    If your manager has commissions enabled, the Payouts tab shows your total earnings. You can filter by today, this week, this month, or all time.

  2. 2
    How your commission is calculated

    Your payout is based on profit, not just revenue. Formula: (Final Sales − Box Cost) × your commission %. Your manager sets your rate. The number is calculated and logged automatically when a stream closes out.

Note: The payout numbers in the app are for tracking only. Your manager pays you directly — Card Break Pro keeps the record.
