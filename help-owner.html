Initial Setup — Do This First

  1. 1
    Add your inventory

    Go to Manage → Inventory. Add every product (box) you carry. Include the cost per box and the product category (e.g. Baseball, Basketball, Football). This is what your breakers select during a live stream.

  2. 2
    Add your breakers

    Go to Manage → Team. Copy the invite code from the top of the page. Send it to your breakers — they go to the login page, click "Breaker? Join with invite code", and create their account. They'll show up in your team list automatically.

  3. 3
    Set commission rates (optional)

    In Manage → Team, toggle commission on and set a rate per breaker. This auto-calculates and deducts from your net profit when streams close out.

  4. 4
    Create your first schedule

    Go to Live → Schedule. Assign shifts to your breakers with a date, time, and stream key. They'll see it in their "My Shifts" tab when they log in.

  5. 5
    You're live

    Your breakers clock in from their account, select products, and submit breaks in real time. You see everything in Reports → Dashboard.

Break Entry — Live Stream Tracking

This is the main screen your breakers use during a live stream. As the owner you can also enter breaks yourself from this tab.

  1. 1
    Enter a Stream Key

    This is the Whatnot event ID or your own label (e.g. "Saturday Night Break"). The system auto-loads any in-progress stream for that key.

  2. 2
    Select products (box cost)

    Use the quantity fields next to each product. The break even and box cost update live. Filter by category using the pill buttons above the table.

  3. 3
    Enter spots and fee %

    Spots = how many slots you sold. Platform Fee % = your Whatnot fee (typically 8%). The Break Even banner will show you the minimum you need to earn — before you even go live.

  4. 4
    Submit breaks as you go

    After each break (each product opened), type the current running Whatnot total and click SUBMIT BREAK. The system calculates each individual break's revenue automatically (new total minus previous total).

  5. 5
    Close Out Stream

    At the end of the stream, enter the final Whatnot sales number and click CLOSE OUT STREAM. This locks in all the numbers, calculates commission, and moves the stream to History.

Tip: The "Current Whatnot Total" field is cumulative — you're typing the running total shown on Whatnot, not each individual break's amount. The app subtracts for you.

Inventory

  1. 1
    Add products

    Name, cost per box, and category. Category is used for filtering during breaks and grouping in Product ROI.

  2. 2
    Set stock quantity

    Track how many boxes you have on hand. This decrements automatically as breaks are submitted.

  3. 3
    Edit and delete

    Click Edit to update cost or category. Deleting a product removes it from future breaks but doesn't affect past history.

Reports & Analytics

📈 Dashboard

Total revenue, net profit, platform fees, and units sold across all closed streams. Profit automatically deducts commission payouts.

📋 Stream Summary

Full breakdown of a single stream — every break, revenue, costs, fees, and final profit for that session.

🗂 Break History

Every individual break logged, filterable by date. See which breaks made money and which didn't.

🏷 Product ROI

Which boxes make you the most money? See revenue, cost, and profit per product across all time.

🏆 Leaderboard

Rank your breakers by revenue generated. Useful for performance reviews and commission conversations.

Team & Commission

  1. 1
    Get the invite code

    Your unique invite code is displayed at the top of the Team tab. Share it with breakers — they use it to create their account from the login page.

  2. 2
    Enable commissions

    Toggle commission on to activate the payout system. Set individual rates per breaker (e.g. 20%). Rates can be changed at any time.

  3. 3
    How commission is calculated

    Payout = (Final Sales − Box Cost) × Commission Rate. This is the profit the breaker generated, times their cut. It gets logged per stream and deducted from your net profit on the Dashboard.

  4. 4
    Payout overview

    The Team tab shows a running total of each breaker's payouts by time period — today, this week, this month, or all time.

Note: Commission payouts are tracked in the system but paid out manually — Card Break Pro does not transfer funds. The numbers are there for your record-keeping.

Schedule

  1. 1
    Create a shift

    Select a breaker, date, time, stream key, and optional notes. Click Schedule Shift. The breaker sees it immediately in their "My Shifts" tab.

  2. 2
    Clock in / Clock out

    When a breaker starts their shift, they clock in from their dashboard — the shift turns yellow (IN PROGRESS). When they close out the stream, they're automatically clocked out.

  3. 3
    Edit or cancel shifts

    Use the Edit and Cancel buttons in the upcoming shifts table. Cancelled shifts remain visible but are marked cancelled.

  4. 4
    Calendar view

    The monthly calendar shows color-coded shift chips. Click the arrows to navigate months. Shifts are color coded by status: blue = scheduled, yellow = in progress, green = completed.

Settings

    Reset Data

    This permanently deletes all breaks, streams, and history for your organization. Use this only if you need to start fresh. This cannot be undone.

Warning: Reset Data is irreversible. Inventory and team accounts are not deleted — only break history and stream data.
